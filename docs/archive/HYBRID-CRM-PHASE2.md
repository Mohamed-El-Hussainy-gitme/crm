# Hybrid CRM - Phase 2

This phase extends the hybrid CRM foundation by finishing the second pass on product UX and operational depth.

## What changed

- Unified the frontend around a shared, calmer design system.
- Upgraded the app shell to Phase 2 branding and cleaner primary/secondary navigation.
- Rebuilt the Contacts workspace with a drawer-based quick-add flow.
- Rebuilt Contact 360 with:
  - unified chronological activity timeline
  - quick-create drawers for tasks, deals, and payments
  - streamlined profile/health rail
  - lighter action handling for notes, calls, tasks, payments, and deals
- Simplified secondary workspaces:
  - Companies
  - Company details
  - Deals
  - Notifications
- Updated the login experience copy to reflect the Phase 2 product direction.

## Design goals achieved

- Keep the surface calm.
- Keep the next action obvious.
- Keep account/deal/admin tooling available without polluting the daily workflow.
- Move creation flows into focused drawers instead of always-on dense forms.

## Verification

The `@smartcrm/web` app was rebuilt successfully after the changes.
